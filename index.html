From the twin Pitons to the warm sandy beaches, St Lucia is full of natural wonders that everyone should see and experience in their life.

Rest in Luxuarious hotels

The Hummingbird Beach Resort is a affordable resort located near the Pitons giving vistors an amazing view from their rooms.

Treat yourself to the luxuarious Royalton Resort, this resort is a all-inclusive destination and hotel rooms over look the stunning carribean sea.

Escape to the Sandals Halcyon Beach Resort for a relaxing holiday located in a tropical garden along the Caribbean sea, making this a memorable vacation you won't forget.

Explore the island

Explore this magical Diamond Botanical Park where a majestic waterfall stands, one of the most popular tourist attractions on the island. This location is both a historic and naturally beautiful site.

The Petit Piton Trail is ideal if you love hiking and nature trails. Throughout this nature trek, you will get to see the beautiful sights as well as finding out more information about the history and culture of the island.

The Treetop Adventure Park is a must do experience, Travel along 12 ziplines through the beautiful rainforest, giving you the bird's eye view of this gorgeoud location.
